## üö® Common Issues & Solutions

### **Problem: Not showing up in searches**
**Solutions:**
- Verify your business (most common issue)
- Complete ALL profile sections
- Add more photos (10+ minimum)
- Get reviews (5+ minimum to start ranking)
- Wait 2-4 weeks after verification

### **Problem: Wrong location showing**
**Solutions:**
- Verify address is correct in Google Business Profile
- Check coordinates match (39.790563, -86.150016)
- Report incorrect location to Google

### **Problem: Competitors ranking higher**
**Solutions:**
- Get more reviews
- Post more frequently
- Add more photos
- Update business hours regularly
- Respond to all reviews quickly
